Welcome!
BuJoApp is an amazing system that keeps a record of everything you could ever want to toss at it. It can be your task manager, sketchbook, journal, idea catcher, and much more! The BuJoApp is a organizational system in your browser that keeps track of anything you would like to in an coherent way. Our app is designed to be simple. We belive that simplicity is a wey to to be focused and productove. BuJoApp base on regular logging and categorizing entries into tasks, events and notes. Tasks are represented by , events by and notes by .
You can add a date to an entry - it will appear in a proper daily and monthly log, or you can leave it without date, then you will find this entry in the backlog.
Every entry can have one of five states: incomplete represented by basic icon, complete represented by , irrelevant - represented by - these entries are also automaticly moved to designed log to not distract you while checking your daily or monthly log. You can always change state of particular entry to move it back to another log. While changing date of the entry, it will be automaticly assigned with , and if you decide to remove date from entry, it will be moved to backlog with icon. If you find one of your tasks more important than other, you can mark it with exclamation mark . A star can be used to emphazise inspirational meaning of entry.
